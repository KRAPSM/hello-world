Global Issue Service Club

Welcome to CAC GISC!

Global Issue Service Club (GISC) tackles issues that we all face such as poverty, global warming, and pollution.

We are here to discuss these problems and learn how to solve them.

Groups of students make a year-long project, or a semester-long project to take actions and change the world.

Examples include a clothing drive, food drive, or donations.

People who are willing to join our club should have responsibility, thus there is a rule in our club.

If you do not come to the meeting more than three times, you are no longer to join our club.

For more information, you are welcome to follow our instagram!

If you have any questions, please email us at hfarouk@cacegypt.org.

Chaperone: Dr Heba Farouk, Ms. Sarah Christensen.

Meeting time: Sundays at lunch.

Meeting Location: Dr. Farouk's room.
